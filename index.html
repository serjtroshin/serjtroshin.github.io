Sergey Troshin

I am an intern deep learning researcher at Bayesian Methods Research Group supervised by Nadezhda Chirkova and Prof. Dmitry Vetrov and a research assistant at the Centre of Deep Learning and Bayesian Methods at the HSE University. I have recently obtained Master’s degree in Computer Science, and I plan to enroll in a PhD program. My research is mainly focused on deep learning for source code. More broadly, I am interested in learning efficient data representations, and natural language processing.
